# MSFF-GAN
MSFF-GAN: Multimodal Spatial-Frequency Fusion for Missing-Modality Brain Tumor MRI Synthesis

We propose MSFF-GAN, a multimodal MRI synthesis framework for missing-modality brain tumor analysis. It employs parameter-decoupled encoders and dual-domain feature fusion to enhance structural consistency and reduce synthesis noise, leading to improved synthesis quality and better downstream segmentation performance.

## Dataset
We conducted empirical experiments on two publicly available datasets, BraTS 2020 and BraTS 2023, to evaluate the effectiveness and robustness of the proposed framework.
